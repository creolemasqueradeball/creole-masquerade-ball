The Meaning of the Mask

For centuries, masquerade traditions have symbolized mystery, elegance, transformation, and celebration.

The mask allows guests to step beyond the ordinary and enter an evening where culture, beauty, music, and fellowship take center stage.

At the Creole Masquerade Ball, the mask serves as a symbol of unity, reminding us that beneath every mask is a unique story and a shared celebration of heritage and community.

The Beginning of a New Tradition

The Creole Masquerade Ball is an inaugural celebration inspired by the elegance, culture, and traditions of Louisiana's Creole heritage.

Held in the heart of New Orleans, this distinguished event brings together guests for an evening of sophistication, fellowship, music, and unforgettable memories.

Every tradition begins with a single moment. The 2027 Creole Masquerade Ball is that moment.
